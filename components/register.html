REGISTER WITH MUT-MMEA

MUT-MMEA is devoted to providing its members with a dependable and secure status within the association through registration. Members are required to pay a registration fee and complete a membership form, which serves the purpose of documentation and provides physical evidence of the member as a registered participant in the association.

The registration fee of KSH 100 is paid through the association's till number.

   After the payment, take the necessary next steps to complete your registration.

A member is required to complete MMMEA membership form upon payment of the registration fee.
