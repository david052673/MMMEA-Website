OUR MEETUPS AND PLANS

January - March 2025 Workplan

Welcome to another exciting semester with MMMEA! Our workplan for January to March 2025 is designed to inspire learning, foster collaboration, and strengthen our community. The activities lined up aim to enhance both technical and personal development while providing opportunities for fun and relaxation.

Brief overview of the scheduled events and what you can expect:

  • Masterclasses on CAD, coding, and electronics to sharpen our technical expertise.
  • Hiking and Fun Day at Monkey Bay for relaxation and bonding.
  • Industrial Visit to Wanjii Kengen Company to gain practical insights into the field of engineering.
  • Faculty Talks, peer tutoring sessions, and guest speakers to inspire and mentor us.
  • Sports Day for teamwork and wellness.
  • Innovation Growth Project Presentations to showcase creativity and collaboration.
  • Wrapping up with an epic end-of-semester bash to celebrate our achievements!
