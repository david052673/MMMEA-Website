PRINCIPLES OF THE ASSOCIATION

Our Association is governed by the following principles:

Ethical conduct and integrity:

Members of the association are supposed to uphold high standards of professionalism, integrity and ethical conduct in all activities

Networking, innovation and creativity:

We encourage innovative thinking and creative problem solving through competitions, design challenges.

Excellence and professionalism:

Members are to embrace hard work and consistency in their academic pursuits to achieve success.

Respect and unity:

Cultivate a sense of unity and camaraderie among members, fostering strong bonds and friendships within the association.

Collaboration and team work:

Emphasizing the importance of collaboration and team work in engineering projects and initiatives.
